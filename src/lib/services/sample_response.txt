<dish_name>Vegetarian Lunch: Vegetable Stir-Fry with Quinoa</dish_name>
<dish_ingredients>100g quinoa, 2 cups water, 1 zucchini, 1 bell pepper, 1 carrot, 1 tablespoon olive oil, 1 clove garlic, 1 tablespoon soy sauce (gluten-free), salt, pepper</dish_ingredients>
<dish_instructions>1. Rinse the quinoa and cook it in 2 cups of water for 15-20 minutes until soft. 2. Slice the zucchini, bell pepper, and carrot into thin strips. 3. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté the garlic. 4. Add the vegetables and stir-fry for 10-12 minutes. 5. Season with gluten-free soy sauce, salt, and pepper. 6. Serve the vegetables over the quinoa.</dish_instructions>

<dish_name>Vegetarian Dinner: Lentil and Vegetable Stew</dish_name>
<dish_ingredients>150g red lentils, 1 onion, 2 cloves garlic, 1 carrot, 1 potato, 1 parsnip, 2 cups gluten-free vegetable broth, 1 can diced tomatoes, 1 teaspoon cumin, 1 teaspoon paprika, salt, pepper</dish_ingredients>
<dish_instructions>1. Rinse the lentils and set aside. 2. Chop the onion, garlic, carrot, potato, and parsnip. 3. In a large pot, sauté the onion and garlic in a little water. 4. Add the chopped vegetables and cook for 5 minutes. 5. Add the lentils, vegetable broth, diced tomatoes, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. 6. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 25-30 minutes until the vegetables and lentils are tender. 7. Serve hot.</dish_instructions>
